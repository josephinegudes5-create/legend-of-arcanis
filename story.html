The Awakening

Long ago, the world of Arcanis was protected by the Guardian Pets and the legendary Adventurers. For hundreds of years, peace filled the kingdoms. But deep beneath the forgotten ruins, an ancient evil known as the Void King has awakened. Now, every new Adventurer must choose a class, bond with a Guardian Pet, and begin a journey to protect the world from darkness. Your legend starts today.
